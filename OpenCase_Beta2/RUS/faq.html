• How to play Jackpot? The max number of weapons that you can add: 15.
    Maximum price is unlimited.
    The game will begin after 50 weapons are collected.
    If you add weapons and close the jackpot, weapons will disappear.
  • Why weapon price is $0? Weapons prices are taken from the database. If there are no prices for current weapon, then price is taken from the Steam Market. If there is no price as well, it counts as $0.
    When the weapon appears in market, open the weapon info in inventory, wait until the price is loaded, reopen inventory.
  • Как обмениваться предметами? First of all, you need to enable "Exchange" in settings.
    How to send the trade offer
    1. Open another player profile
    2. Press on the exchange icon ()
    3. Select the weapons that you want to send and press on the "Next" button
    4. Select the weapons that you want to get from player (WIP)
    5. If you want to send weapons as a gift then check "It\'s a gift" checkbox
    6. Press on the "Send trade" button
    How to get weapons
    1. Open your profile
    2. Press on the exchange icon ()
    3. Select the player that send the offer to you
    4. Select the offer
    5. Change your offer if you want
    6. Check the "Check this box when ready to trade." checkbox
    7. Wait until another player check this checkbox too and press on the "Make trade" button. Or another player can press it
    If another player pressed the "Make trade" button when you was offline then open this trade to get your weapons.'
  • How to level up? For increasing your level you need points. Points are given for the following steps:
    • Open the app - ${1} points (award is given once a day)
    • Open one case - ${2} point
    • Won in the mini-game - ${3} points
